AI industry applicability assessment system

This system evaluates the applicability of AI in specific industries based on three core dimensions:

  • technology suitability: Evaluate the feasibility of technology implementation
  • economic feasibility: Evaluate return on investment and cost-effectiveness
  • social feasibility: Assessing policy support and public acceptance

Please enter industry information and adjust various indicators on the right, and the system will automatically calculate the evaluation results and display them in 3D space.
